Publications

This collection presents selected peer-reviewed publications from the BAHE Knowledge Network. Each publication is accompanied by curated summaries, knowledge statements and links to related media and research resources to allow exploring both the publication and its broader scientific context. We welcome constructive discussion, collaboration and feedback that support ecological research and the continued development of the BAHE Knowledge Network.

Optimizing Biodiversity Offsetting for Habitat Succession and Colonization Dynamics

Konstans Wells, Luca Börger, Miguel Lurgi, Kevin Watts, James M. Bullock
Conservation Biology · 2026

This study provides a quantitative ecological framework for improving biodiversity offset policy by demonstrating that robust no-net-loss outcomes require larger compensation areas, long-term habitat protection and explicit incorporation of ecological recovery, succession and landscape dynamics into offset design.
