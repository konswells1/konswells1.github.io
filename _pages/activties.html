Activities

Our activities go beyond research publications — we contribute through teaching, training, outreach, and engagement with diverse communities. Below you can explore current and past highlights.

Teaching

We are committed to high-quality teaching and mentoring, sharing knowledge across undergraduate, postgraduate, and professional levels. This includes:

  • Undergraduate and Master’s courses in ecology, conservation, and data science.
  • Supervision of Bachelor’s, Master’s, and PhD projects.
  • Workshops on computational and experimental methods.

Updates and detailed teaching activities will be added soon.

Outreach & Engagement

Our lab is actively involved in outreach and engagement to share science and inform practice. Activities include:

  • Collaboration with policymakers and conservation practitioners.
  • Public talks, workshops, and school visits.
  • Media engagement and contributions to science communication.
